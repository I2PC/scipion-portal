Scipion

Cryo EM image proccessing framework. Integration, traceability and analysis.

Download

Integration of Cryo em software

Scipion does not do any image processing as such. It integrates existing software allowing our users to, smoothly, switch form one software to another offering endless combinations. It started with SPA methods but currently it reaches also modelling software, and tomography is about to come.

Developed by ...

Scipion started in Madrid, but nowadays its development is distributed among different academic institutions. Although the "core" of Scipion is developed in a few places, Scipion has become a "framework" ready to accept "new packages" (plugins) on the fly, therefore, there are already many people involved and we hope more people will join this project.
CNB - Instruct Image Processing Center

Located in Madrid were Scipion started. It is the home of Xmipp, a classic in cryo em image processing software and one of the main locations were Scipion is produced.

http://i2pc.es
SciLifeLab

Located in Stockholm, lots of Scipion core contributions, facilities betatesting and plugin developments are coming from there.

For cryo electron microscopy facilities

Scipion has been designed to process images on-the-fly while your microscope is being acquiring images. You decide what methods to run without the need of any programming skills, everything from the GUI (graphical user interface).
